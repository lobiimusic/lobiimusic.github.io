BIOGRAPHY

Hailing from New Orleans, Louisiana, LOBII established his moniker in 2016 during his freshman year of college at Loyola University: New Orleans. Known for his prominent use of sampling, earth-shattering bass frequencies, and lush and video-game-like synths, Carter’s influences range from a multitude of different artists and musical styles. Crediting his inspiration from artists such as Holy Other, Lapalux, Shlohmo, and Galimatias, to artists such as Kimbra, Björk, The Acid and Sun Glitters, Carter has developed a unique form of music that combines left-field hip-hop, electronic, indie and pop music all into one symbiotic and dream-like genre. LOBII has released his first official single, “Something to Cry To”, and will be bringing more to the world of electronic music soon.
